Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate cookies are a favorite of mine, and I wanted to share a recipe of how to make them. It's very simple and won't take much time at all, but is delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolqte chips

Instructions:

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix the flour and sugar together in a bowl. Mix in eggs and then flour until combined. Mix in chocolate chips.
  3. Roll into little balls and put onto a baking sheet, 2 inches apart. Bake in oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Let them cool and enjoy!
